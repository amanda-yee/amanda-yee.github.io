About Me

I currently reside in New York but I was born in New Zealand and grew up on the Gold Coast in Australia. I made the move to the US in November 2019 for work. I initially lived in Chicago and was travelling weekly to Arkansas for that job until the COVID-19 pandemic hit. I have since made the move to New York and have lived in Brooklyn for two years now. For the longest time it has been a dream to live in New York so I feel very fortunate that it has become my reality.

I completed a Bachelors in Actuarial Science and with that degree I have worked as a data scientist for the past five years. I am currently enrolled at Boston University pursuing a Masters in Software Development. Upon completion of my Masters degree I hope to use the knowledge gained to pivot to a career as a front end web developer. My first exposure to coding was frequently changing my MySpace profile by editing the source code so becoming a web developer will be a full circle moment! One of my favorite things to change was the song that would play when someone would visit my profile.

With that, this is one of my current favorite songs:
